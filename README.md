🚀 How to Add "Gif Giver" to your Microsoft Teams Sidebar


Want to search and copy GIFs directly inside Microsoft Teams? You can add this website as a permanent app on your Teams sidebar in just a few minutes. No coding required!


What you need before you start:


Copy this website link to your clipboard: https://jessbhamra.github.io/gif-giver/


Step 1: Open the Teams Developer Portal
Open Microsoft Teams.
Click on the Apps icon on the far left sidebar.
Search for Developer Portal and click to open it. (If you don't have it, click "Add" to install it).


Step 2: Create Your App
Inside the Developer Portal, click the Apps tab at the top.
Click the + New app button.
Type Gif Giver as the name and click Add.


Step 3: Fill in the Blanks
You will now see a "Basic Information" page. Fill out these specific boxes:
Short description: Search and copy GIFs for Teams.
Full description: A quick tool to search Giphy and copy animated GIFs directly into Microsoft Teams chats.
Developer Name: Feel free to blame it on Jess
Website, Privacy, and Terms of Use: Scroll down to the URLs section. Paste the website link (from the top of this guide) into all three of these boxes.
Click Save at the bottom.


Step 4: Connect the Website
Look at the left-hand menu and click on App features.
Click on the Personal app card.
Click Create your first personal app tab.
Name it Search.
In the Content URL box, paste the website link again.
Click Confirm.


Step 5: Install and Pin!
Look at the top right corner of the screen and click Preview in Teams.
A window will pop up introducing your new app. Click Add.
Gif Giver will now open on your left sidebar!
Pro Tip: Right-click the new Gif Giver icon on your sidebar and select Pin so it never disappears.
That's it! You can now click the icon anytime to search for GIFs, click "Copy", and paste them directly into your chats.
